Description

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. It has a diverse environment that includes vast oceans, towering mountains, deep valleys, and dense forests. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water, while the remaining 29% consists of continents and islands. The atmosphere, made up mostly of nitrogen and oxygen, plays a vital role in protecting life by blocking harmful solar radiation and maintaining a stable climate.
Earth also has a dynamic interior, with layers such as the crust, mantle, and core, which contribute to geological activities like earthquakes and volcanic eruptions. It rotates on its axis, leading to day and night, and revolves around the Sun, which causes seasonal changes. Earth supports a wide variety of ecosystems and species, making it a unique and precious planet in the solar system. Its balanced conditions and natural resources are essential for human survival and development.

Interesting Facts About Earth

  • Earth’s atmosphere is mostly nitrogen and oxygen.
  • It has one moon, which influences tides and stabilizes its rotation.
  • Earth’s surface temperature varies widely, supporting diverse climates.
  • The planet completes one rotation every 24 hours.
